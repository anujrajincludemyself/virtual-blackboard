ğŸ”¬ TECHNICAL CONCEPTS (Thoda Detail Mein)

1. CANVAS:
   - Ek "invisible black board" hai jo background mein hai
   - Jab aap draw karte ho, ye canvas pe save hota hai
   - Camera image ke upar overlay ho jata hai

2. LANDMARKS:
   - Haath ke 21 specific points hote hain
   - Har point ki position (x, y) coordinates mein milti hai
   - Example: Point #8 = Index finger tip

3. FINGER DETECTION:
   - Program check karta hai ki finger tip, finger middle joint se upar hai ya neeche
   - Upar = Finger UP (1)
   - Neeche = Finger DOWN (0)

4. 3D PROJECTION:
   - 3D shapes ko 2D screen pe dikhane ke liye "perspective projection" use hoti hai
   - Jaise rail ki patri door jakar chhoti dikhti hai, same concept!

5. ROTATION MATRICES:
   - 3D shapes ko ghumane ke liye mathematical formulas
   - X-axis, Y-axis, Z-axis pe rotation

6. LINE DRAWING:
   - Previous position (xp, yp) aur current position (x1, y1) ko connect karo
   - Continuous lines banti hain
